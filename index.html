About Me

Hello, my name is Enmanuel, and I am from Nicaragua. I have 4 years of experience as an accountant and interpreter and two additional years of experience as a software engineer. I have been providing translation services to medical professionals and patients in the United States, the United Kingdom, and Canada since 2021. As an accountant, I managed administrative tasks, and as a medical interpreter, I facilitated communication and appointment scheduling.

In my role as a software engineer, I have worked on a range of projects including bookstore websites, budget applications, inventory management systems, appointment setters, clocks, and markdown previewers. I have experience in front-end and back-end development, with a strong focus on web technologies like React, TypeScript, and Vite. My goal is to further develop my skills in software engineering and explore new opportunities in the tech industry to create innovative solutions.
